Places I like to Visit in Saudi Arabia By: Faisal Almannaei

Saudi Arabia is a desert country encompassing most of the Arabian Peninsula, with Red Sea and Persian (Arabian) Gulf coastlines. Also, Saudi Arabia or as known as (KSA) is the largest country in the Arabian gulf states. Saudi Arabia has one of the largest companies in the world by revenue (Saudi Aramco), according to accounts seen by Bloomberg News, it is the most profitable company in the world.
